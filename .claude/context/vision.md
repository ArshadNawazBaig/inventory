# Vision

> **Status:** 🟡 Seed · **Owner:** Product/CTO · **Related:** [PRD §1](../../docs/PRODUCT_REQUIREMENTS.md)

## Vision statement
> To become the inventory system of record that mid-market businesses actually enjoy using —
> combining the correctness and control of an enterprise ERP with the speed, clarity, and
> beauty of a modern productivity tool.

## The false choice we eliminate
Inventory software forces a choice between *powerful* and *pleasant*. We refuse it. A warehouse
operator receives a shipment in seconds on a phone; a CFO trusts every number in an audit; an
admin defines exactly who can do what — all in one fast, beautiful, multi-tenant platform.

## Three product truths
1. **Stock accuracy is the product.** Everything else serves "screen = shelf."
2. **Inventory is multi-dimensional** (variant × location × lot/serial × status).
3. **It is a system of record — trust is paramount** (audit, RBAC, isolation from day one).

## North Star
**Weekly Active Stock Movements** per active org — the measure of being the real system of
record, not a passive viewer.
